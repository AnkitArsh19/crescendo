Crescendo — Workflow Automation Platform

Crescendo is a workflow automation platform that helps you build, orchestrate, and monitor complex workflows with an intuitive visual builder. Connect your services, schedule tasks, and let Crescendo handle the rest.

Key Features

  • Visual Workflow Builder — Design workflows with a drag-and-drop interface
  • App Integrations — Connect Google Sheets, Gmail, Discord, Slack, and more
  • Execution History — Monitor and debug workflow runs in real time
  • Email Service — Send transactional emails with built-in analytics
  • Secure OAuth — Connect your accounts securely via OAuth 2.0

Why Crescendo Requests Access to Your Google Account

Crescendo requests access to your Google account data (such as Google Sheets, Gmail, Google Drive, and Google Calendar) solely to execute the workflow automations you configure. For example, if you create a workflow that reads data from a Google Sheet and sends an email via Gmail, Crescendo needs authorized access to those specific Google services to perform those actions on your behalf. We do not use your Google data for advertising, analytics, or any purpose other than providing the workflow automation services you request.
